Tips

  • Keep labels concise and placed close to inputs.
  • Use aria-describedby for helper text or instructions.
  • Prefer semantic layout and accessible media captions/alt text.

This demo emphasizes semantic HTML, accessibility and native HTML5 validation. All form inputs are for demo only — the action="#" attribute means this form will not actually submit to a server.
